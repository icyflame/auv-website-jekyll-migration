SPONSORS

To achieve success in our goal, our team relies on the generous support of our sponsors. Your kind donation will help us in purchasing costly sensors, hardware and fabrication of the AUV. Sponsorship money will also support us in participating in the International ROBOSUB Competition to be held in San Diego, USA(eg: Travel support for team members).We would like to thank all our sponsors for supporting the technological development in underwater Robotics . We will strive to provide special benefits to our sponsor and would like to continue long term association.

Why Sponsor Us?

  • Promoting robotics research among undergraduate students.
  • We are one of the 4 Indian Teams, that participate in international ROBOSUB, held at San Diego each year.
  • We were the winners of SAVe 2011(Student AUV competition) organized by NIOT, Ministry of Earth Sciences, Government of India.
  • AUV shall be a big boost to your Corporate Social Responsibility, for supporting and nurturing a national level team which represents our country internationally.

Sponsorship Benefits

  • Company visibility at the AUVSI/ONR ROBOSUB competition held in San Diego every year.
  • Special Distinction in media coverage
  • Company logo on competition vehicle and posters
  • Company information and logo on the website
  • Company name on the team uniform
  • An Asscociation with IIT Kharagpur
  • Recognition in time-to-time workshops and exhibitions by team at notable events such as the Annual Alumni Meet of IIT Kharagpur.

ABOUT US

The IIT Kharagpur Autonomous Underwater Vehicle Team (Team AUV) is a student group working in the field of autonomous underwater robotics. We design and build fully autonomous underwater robots for research and competitive purposes.
